Rank The Adjectives The Sizes, Ranked About This Site

Objective:

This website comes with the lofty goal of creating a rough scale for every size-modifying adjective in the English language. "Huge" is obviously larger than "big", but is "colossal" larger than "titanic"?

Methodology:

This site keeps two separate lists for large and small adjectives. A comparative Elo system (currently at K=32) is used to rank and compare each word. Elo is statistically limited in its capacity to accurately predict the results of competitions, but should be good enough for most purposes, and still sees wide use to this day.

Limitations:

Dataset may not actually contain every size-modifying adjective used in the English language. May or may not be statistically significant enough to settle a bar argument. Inquires and requests for adjective additions may be admitted for adjudication at: email@gmail.com
